Baby Registries!

As a mother myself, I can tell you what are must haves on your baby registry. Have a baby they said.. it will be fun they said.. Raising a child can be very daunting idea, but if you have the right mindset and proper tools; parenthood can be a little more manageable. There are some things that are game-changing. And others not worth the expensive price-tag. I curated a list of essential needs as well as a few items to avoid.

What is a baby registry?

A baby registry is a list of items from a local store or online shop that you would like to receive at your baby shower or before your baby's birth.

List of essential baby items:

  1. crib
  2. car seat
  3. stroller
  4. baby carrier
  5. pacifiers
  6. baby bottles
  7. onesies
  8. bibs
  9. diapers
  10. diaper bag
  11. sound machine

Honorable Mentions

  • baby rocker
  • changing mats
  • rocking chair

Unneccesary Baby Items for Newborn baby

  • wipe warmer
  • baby shoes
  • slip proof knee pads
  • velcro swaddles
  • crib bumpers
